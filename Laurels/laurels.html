The Wharton Global High School Investment Competition is an online investment simulation for high school students. The students of Indian Language school, The Financial Miners team have made it to the regional finals of this competition. From over 3000 teams across 78 countries, the team has made it to the regionals with 49 other teams to compete for the globals.
